Our Story

Bradner Countryside Smokehouse was born from a passion for preserving traditional smoking techniques while serving the Fraser Valley community with exceptional quality products.

Located in Abbotsford, British Columbia, we specialize in artisanal smoked meats using time-honored methods that have been refined over generations. Our commitment to quality means we source only the finest local ingredients and take the time necessary to create products that exceed expectations.

What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted source for both retail customers and wholesale partners throughout the Fraser Valley, including Aldergrove, Langley, Chilliwack, and Mission.

Our Commitment

Quality First: We use only premium cuts of meat and the finest seasonings to ensure every product meets our high standards.
Traditional Methods: Our smoking process follows time-tested techniques that bring out the natural flavors of each cut.
Local Community: We're proud to serve the Fraser Valley and support local businesses with our wholesale services.

Our Smoking Process

Selection

We carefully select premium cuts of meat from trusted local suppliers, ensuring quality from the very beginning.

Preparation

Each cut is prepared with our signature rubs and seasonings, developed through years of experimentation and refinement.

Smoking

Using traditional smoking methods and premium wood, we slowly smoke each product to achieve the perfect flavor and texture.
